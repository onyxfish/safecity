h3=. Completely Anonymous

p. *To get text messages warning you of danger*
Text *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}on* and your location
Example: *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}on austin and lake*
Example: *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}on 100 n austin*

To stop getting messages text *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}off*

p. *To warn others of danger*
Text where you are and what you see
Example: *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}i saw a man with a gun at austin and lake*
Example: *{background-color:lightgray;font-family:monospace;font-size:12px}a lady got her purse stolen at 100 n austin*
